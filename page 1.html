Kill Zone for Mobile Signal

Forums have expressed that the mobile network switches to the Malaysia region in Palau Ubin, with some spots having no internet connection altogether. Do remember to switch off your data roaming if your internet plan is not international in case of extra charges. Wifi is only available at the Celestial Resort, the only resort in Pulau Ubin.

Residents call it 'cemetery road'

The trails for cycling in Pulau Ubin are not fully paved, many of which lead to rocky dirt roads that have a higher tendency for tripping. Paved roads are also subjected to weathering, leading to unexpected cracks and dents. Thankfully, some of the more notorious stretches of road have road signs, humps and convex mirrors where there are blind corners.'Steep slope ahead', 'Slow' or 'Dismount', the signs warn, but cyclists are unfazed. Ubin villagers count an average of 10 serious cycling accidents a month, all in the same hot spots - Jalan Batu Ubin, the stretch near Belatok Hut, Jalan Mamam, the road leading to Chek Jawa Reserve and Jalan Wat Siam or 'Cemetery Hill'.
